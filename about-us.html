ABOUT US

Welcome to Romofy Agro an eCommerce website

We are a Rajasthan-based company that focuses on producing Fully Certified (NPOP NOP) organic food using traditional methods, which means we use the minimum possible amount of external inputs like pesticides and chemical fertilizers. We are committed to providing a pure and wholesome food source to people worldwide. We have our offices in Mumbai & Rajasthan & ICS in multiple parts of India We are one of the leading manufacturers and suppliers of organic food. We have been working in this direction for many years,

and we specialize in the supply of organic & IPM food in bulk quantities We are proud to own our 10 ICS with 5000 plus farmers and be associated with more than 2500 organic certified & IPM farmers by means of backward integration and contract farming. We endeavor to ensure that each of our clients is satisfied with our product quality. We are working with the intention and enthusiasm to provide our products with 100% transparency and traceability. We assure you that it will be a great experience for you to work with us. Thank you for your time we have attached herewith the necessary documents like Scope (NPOP, NOP),

OID Certificate, and Ready to dispatch Material List, for your reference, please go through the same and update us. Also please let us know if you want to visit our premises, ICS, or warehouses for an inspection of product quality or any other purpose. We would love to know your requirements or queries related to any Organic, IPM, Conventional Spices, Oil Seeds, Herbs, or any other food products to start our long terms business partnership as soon as possible.
